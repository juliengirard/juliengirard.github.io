Julien H. Girard

astronomer

Time lapse by Dominique Joubert 2015

Physicist and astronomer, I am currently an AURA Scientist at the Space Telescope Science Institute (STScI) in Baltimore.
I study exoplanetary systems using high contrast, direct imaging and spectroscopy techniques.
Functionally I lead coronagraphy efforts on two NASA flagship missions: JWST and Roman.
